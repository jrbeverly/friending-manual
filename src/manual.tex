\section{Account}
\subsection{Signing up for your account}
\setDefaultMenu
To start using \Friending, create an account by opening the mobile application, \Friending. You will be presented with the \Screenshot{Start} as shown in Figure~\ref{fig:start}. \action{Tap} on the \keys{Start the tour} button.

\appscreenshot{Sign Up}{\appSignUp}{fig:signup}

Then, on the \Screenshot{\appSignUp} (Figure~\ref{fig:signup}), \action{enter} your \txtinput{email}, \txtinput{name} and \txtinput{password}.  You should review the \txtexternal{Terms of Service} and \txtexternal{Privacy Policy} that define the expectations for you when using \Friending. Afterwards, \action{tap} on the \keys{Sign Up} button.
